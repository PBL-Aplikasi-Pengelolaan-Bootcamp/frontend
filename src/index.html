Apart from that, this profession is considered to have future prospects. The words "cool" and "big salary" are often thrown at programmers. This is certainly very interesting, especially for those of you who want to feel the sweetness of becoming a skilled programmer. Not only that, programmers also have work relationships such as IT support, graphic designer, data analyst, digital marketing, teacher and etc
